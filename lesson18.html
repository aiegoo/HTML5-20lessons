Understanding Codecs

  • The term codecs stands for encoder/decoder and describes the functionality provided by software that encodes and decodes media such as audio and video.
  • Below are the codecs currently in use by HTML5 audio tag:
    AAC (Advanced Audio Coding)
    MP3
    PCM(Pluse Coded Modulation)
    Vorbis(Ogg Vorbis)- .ogg extension
  • following list details the major operating systems and browsers alongwith the audio types they support by default:
    • Apple iOS - AAC, MP3,PCM
    • Apple Safari - AAC, MP3,PCM
    • Google Android 2.3+ - AAC,MP3,Vorbis
    • Internet Explorer - AAC,MP3
    • Mozilla Firefox - MP3,PCM,Vorbis
    • Opera- PCM,Vorbis
