An Introduction to HTML

What is HTML:
* HTML stands for-Hyper Text Markup Language
* invented by Sir Timothy Berners-Lee in the early 1990s to solve the problem of quixkly and efficiently distributing documents between scientists around he world who were working with experimenters at CERN

USES:
* Any computer to stay in touch with each other, a click away.
* People all over the world could connect to a local Internet host and immediately be in touch with any person logged in to any web-connected computer.


HTTP AND HTML BASICS:

1. HTTP- It is the communication standard used for controlling the requests and responses that occur between a web browser running on your computer and a web server, and stands for hyperText Transfer Protocol.
